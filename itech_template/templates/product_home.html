Banking

ITech's success in the financial industry is based on a clear vision of what our clients need to increase their market share, profits and productivity and our ability to deliver proven results.

The financial services industries have grown tremendously in past two decades, spawning many challenges and rising through a competitive edge. Deregulation forced banks to be more competitive and enter into entire gamut of services including retail, investor and wholesale services.

ITech understands the banking requirement and we are committed to address the unique needs of banks. ITech brings you the competitive advantage with the following objective:

  • To satisfy new regulatory environment (privacy, accounting and so on)
  • To improve business process
  • To improve customer quality through IT


ITech's buffet of financial service consists of domain experts, technical consultants and business analysts who have been proved to establish the excellence in service by addressing specific pain areas in the industry.
